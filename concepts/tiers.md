# Tiers

Ramoira supports a split between:

- **Local / private schemas** (full policy detail for internal agents)
- **Public / shareable schemas** (summary fields suitable for public distribution)

In practice:

- full schemas contain commercial + governance details that are often strategically sensitive
- summary schemas contain enough identity/narrative/voice signal for representation accuracy

The exact tiering and publication model is product-defined and may vary by deployment.
